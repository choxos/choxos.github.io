About Me

I hold a Doctor of Dental Surgery (DDS) degree from Tehran University of Medical Sciences (2020) and a Master of Public Health (MPH) from King's College London (2023). My work focuses on meta-research, where I assess the transparency, reproducibility, and risk of bias in health research. I have also been actively involved in projects related to evidence-based dentistry, public health, and data science. My goal is to contribute to a more reliable and transparent research ecosystem.
